# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This Excel dashboard provides a concise, business-focused view of sales performance across regions, products, time periods, and sales personnel. It is designed to help business stakeholders quickly monitor key metrics, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Objective
The primary objective of this dashboard is to:
- Track overall sales performance and volume over time  
- Compare performance across regions, products, and sales personnel  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming segments  
- Enable quick insights through interactive filtering and KPIs  

## Data Scope
The dashboard is built on transactional sales data with the following key dimensions and measures:
- **Dimensions:** Region, Product, Month, Sales Person  
- **Measures:** Sales Quantity, Sales Value, Net Value, GST-Inclusive Value  

## Key KPIs
- **Total Sales Value:** Indicates overall revenue performance for the selected period and filters  
- **Total Sales Quantity:** Reflects sales volume and demand trends  

These KPIs provide an immediate snapshot of business health and are dynamically updated based on user selections.

## Dashboard Components & Insights

### Month-wise Slicer
- Enables time-based analysis and period comparisons  
- Helps identify seasonal patterns and monthly performance shifts  

### Sales Person Slicer
- Allows evaluation of individual or team-level performance  
- Supports performance benchmarking and incentive planning  

### Sales by Region (Bar Chart)
- Highlights regional contribution to total sales  
- Identifies strong and weak geographic markets for targeted action  

### Month-wise Sales Trend (Column Chart)
- Visualizes sales growth or decline over time  
- Helps detect trends, volatility, and potential forecasting signals  

### Sales by Product Category (Pie Chart)
- Shows product mix and revenue contribution by category  
- Helps identify core products and opportunities for portfolio optimization  

## Business Value Delivered
- **Faster Decision-Making:** Centralized KPIs and visuals reduce analysis time  
- **Performance Transparency:** Clear visibility into regional, product, and sales personnel performance  
- **Trend Identification:** Month-wise analysis supports planning and forecasting  
- **Strategic Focus:** Helps management prioritize high-impact regions and products  

## Intended Audience
- Sales Managers  
- Business Analysts  
- Regional Heads  
- Senior Management  

## Conclusion
This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, uncover trends, and make informed strategic decisions with confidence.
